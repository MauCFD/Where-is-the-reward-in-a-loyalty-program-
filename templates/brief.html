What Is a Rewards Program?

Rewards programs are a great way to engage customers' attention. They work by offering prizes, discounts, and even cash to their most loyal buyers to retain them. The more often a client consumes a brand, the more benefits will be available. For a Rewards Program to be successful in a company's strategy, theory says it shall return no more than 2% of its total income.
The next project will consider a shock absorber brand's program. Its mechanics are simple. Subscribed customers are categorized by their monthly level of purchase, and these 6 different categories will serve to establish a minimum level of sales to be reached. This will serve to obtain monthly points, but by the quarter's end, a couple of bonuses can also be gained. Ultimately category C6 can earn an additional bonus at the end of the year as well.
